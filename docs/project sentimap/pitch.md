# SentiMap 

Good day everyone. SentiMap is a hybrid NLP and geospatial analytics system that transforms Cebu City traffic enforcement grievances into actionable intelligence. Public complaints are scattered across social media, written in Cebuano/Bislish, and filled with slang and sarcasm, which causes generic sentiment tools to misread them; SentiMap fixes this by scraping Reddit through custom v2 and godmode scripts, validating relevance with a human-in-the-loop pipeline, and precomputing Cebuano-aware sentiment, sarcasm, and multi-label emotions. It then extracts locations with confidence weighting, clusters grievances into hotspots, and serves a real-time dashboard that shows an interactive heatmap, a grievance feed, and analytics like emotion distribution, severity mix, sarcasm vs direct, dominant emotions, and a database-driven category breakdown. The impact is practical: authorities can identify hotspot locations, prioritize the most severe and sarcastic complaints, understand emotional patterns (anger, frustration, fear, disgust, resignation, trust), and use evidence-backed summaries to optimize enforcement and policy decisions. SentiMap is a complete pipeline from data collection to actionable geospatial insight, and it is ready for thesis defense and real-world deployment in Cebu City.
